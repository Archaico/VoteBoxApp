## On-Chain Voting Data

Proposals and votes recorded on the Cardano blockchain are public by the nature of
the blockchain itself. No additional licence is required to read or archive this
data. Any derived analytics products built on top of this public data are governed
separately and are not part of this repository.

## Protocol Fee Transparency

VoteBoxApp collects a small ADA fee on proposal submission. A fixed percentage of
this fee is allocated to the founder as a Perpetual Founder Fee (PFF), with the
remainder flowing to the DAO community treasury. This allocation is an immutable
constant encoded in the on-chain smart contract — it is publicly auditable by
anyone and cannot be changed without community visibility. This is a deliberate
design choice: revenue sustainability is built into the open protocol, not hidden
behind proprietary code.
